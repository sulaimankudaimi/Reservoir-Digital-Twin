## üõ†Ô∏è Technical Capabilities & Features

### 1. **3D Structural & Fluid Dynamics (The Digital Twin)**
- **Spatial Modeling:** Real-time 3D rendering of reservoir topography using geological surface mapping.
- **Pressure Heatmapping:** Dynamic visualization of reservoir energy. 
    - **Red Zones:** High-pressure virgin zones (Prime targets for new wells).
    - **Blue Zones:** Depletion cones (Monitoring production drawdown and recovery efficiency).

[Image of 3D reservoir pressure heatmap showing drawdown zones around wells]

### 2. **Advanced Petrophysical Interpretation**
- **Well Log Analytics:** Direct processing of subsurface data (CSV/LAS) to identify pay zones.
- **Lithology Mapping:** Visualizing Gamma Ray and Resistivity logs to differentiate between Sandstone (Reservoir) and Shale (Seal).

[Image of typical oil well log curves including Gamma Ray and Resistivity with lithology interpretation]

### 3. **AI-Driven Forecasting Engine**
- **Decline Curve Analysis (DCA):** Utilizing Machine Learning to predict future production trends and pressure decline rates.
- **Predictive Horizon:** Interactive "Time-Travel" feature to visualize reservoir state up to 10 years in the future.

[Image of a production decline curve with historical data and machine learning forecast]
